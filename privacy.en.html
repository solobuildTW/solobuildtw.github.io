Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

SoloBuildTW ("we", "us", or "the site") takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Types of Information Collected

When you purchase products or interact with us, we may collect the following information:

  • Name and email address
  • Payment information (processed via NewebPay or Lemon Squeezy; we do not store credit card numbers)
  • Purchase history and order information
  • Browsing behavior and device information (collected via cookies and Google Analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Processing your orders and delivering digital products
  • Responding to customer service inquiries and providing after-sales support
  • Sending order confirmations and product update notifications
  • Analyzing website traffic and user behavior to improve service quality

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This site uses the following tracking technologies:

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4, Tracking ID: G-MFM38NPEGE): Used for analyzing website traffic, user behavior, and conversions. GA4 collects anonymized browsing data including page views, session duration, and device type.
  • Essential cookies: Used to maintain basic site functionality such as language preferences.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

4. Data Protection Measures

  • The entire site uses HTTPS encrypted transmission
  • Payment information is processed directly by third-party payment services (NewebPay, Lemon Squeezy); we never handle or store credit card data
  • Only authorized personnel can access customer personal data

5. Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • NewebPay — Credit card payment processing for Taiwan
  • Lemon Squeezy — International credit card payment processing
  • Google Analytics — Website traffic analytics
  • GitHub Pages — Website hosting service

6. Your Rights

Under the Personal Data Protection Act, you have the following rights:

  • Inquire about or request access to your personal data
  • Request supplementation or correction of your personal data
  • Request cessation of collection, processing, or use of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

7. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page without individual notification. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.
