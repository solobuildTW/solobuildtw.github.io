FAQ

Questions shops ask

I'm not technical can I use this?

Yes. No code, no prompts to write. The 4-tab dashboard is designed for non-technical shop owners. Upload chat exports, teach in plain Chinese. 30 min to get started. Free Zoom onboarding for the first month.

How does my Line OA connect?

You give us your Line OA Channel Secret + Access Token (5 min to get from Line Developers Console). We hook it up in 30 min. See BUSINESS_LINE_OA_GUIDE.md for the step-by-step.

Is my shop a good fit?

All shops fit — restaurants / cafes / BBQ stalls / beauty / nails / pet grooming / tutoring / clinics / studios / retail / B&B / gyms / photography / clothing / bakeries / B2B vendors. If your customers ask questions on Line you should have a 24-hour AI to answer.

Will customers know it's AI?

Default policy: not pretending to be human. When asked "are you AI" the bot will say "Yes I'm the Line Auto-Reply Bot". Replies are natural and conversational, no fake human personas.

What happens on customer complaints?

The bot detects keywords like "complaint" "refund" "human please" and instantly forwards to your personal Line. The bot does NOT try to handle disputes on its own.

Can it handle bookings?

Yes. Customer says "I want to book tomorrow 6pm 4 people" → bot creates booking → pushes notification to your Line → you confirm → bot replies customer. Q3 will add Google Calendar / Cal.com sync.

Is there a conversation cap?

Monthly tier includes a conversation cap far above what small shops use in a year. If you ever hit a peak season we'll auto-notify and offer top-up packs. Never service interruption.

What AI model does it use?

DeepSeek V3 — best Chinese model on the market, most cost-effective for Taiwan shops. Enterprise plans can swap to GPT-4 / Claude.

Is my customer data safe?

Supabase PostgreSQL + Row Level Security. Each shop's data fully isolated. Stored in ap-southeast-1 (Singapore). GDPR / TW PDPA compliant.

Refund policy?

Monthly within 7 days. Half-year / Annual within 30 days pro-rata refund of remaining time. 14-day trial period is stop-anytime no charge.

FB / IG too?

FB Messenger Bot is on the roadmap (Q3). IG support is in planning, pending Meta DM API policy clarity. For now we are focused on making the Line experience first-class.
